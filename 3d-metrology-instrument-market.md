<p><strong>The 3D Metrology Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM)</li><li>Optical Digitizer and Scanner (ODS)</li><li>Video Measuring Machine (VMM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Metrology Instrument Market includes three primary types: Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs), Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODS), and Video Measuring Machines (VMM). CMMs utilize probing technologies to provide precise dimensional measurements of physical objects, making them essential for quality control in manufacturing. ODS employs optical methods to capture 3D shapes and surface features, ideal for complex geometries. VMMs combine optical and video technology to enhance measurement accuracy and speed, focusing on 2D and 3D dimensional inspections.</p></p>
