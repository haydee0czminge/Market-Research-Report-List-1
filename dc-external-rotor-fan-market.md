<p><p>The DC external rotor fan market primarily encompasses three types: axial flow fans, centrifugal fans, and other specialized designs. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan axis, offering high efficiency and low noise levels, making them ideal for cooling applications. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, utilize a rotating impeller to draw air in and expel it at a perpendicular angle, providing higher pressure and airflow for applications demanding robust air movement. Other market types may include miscelaneous fans tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
