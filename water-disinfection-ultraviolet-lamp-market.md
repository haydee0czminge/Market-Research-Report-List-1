<p><p>The water disinfection ultraviolet lamp market is categorized into three types based on pressure: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low-pressure lamps emit UV-C light at a single wavelength, making them efficient for small-scale applications. Medium-pressure lamps produce a broader spectrum of UV light, suitable for larger volume disinfection needs. High-pressure lamps operate at elevated pressure, delivering high-intensity UV light and are often used in industrial applications due to their effectiveness in treating large quantities of water quickly.</p></p>
