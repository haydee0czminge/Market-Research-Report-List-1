<p><p>The trenchless machinery market primarily includes Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) machines and Pipe Jacking machines. HDD machines facilitate the installation of pipelines and cables underground by drilling a pilot hole along a predetermined path, minimizing surface disruption. Conversely, Pipe Jacking machines are utilized for pushing pipes through the ground using a series of jacks, ideal for shorter distances and urban environments. Both technologies enhance efficiency and reduce the environmental impact associated with traditional excavation methods.</p></p>
