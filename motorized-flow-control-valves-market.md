<p><p>Motorized flow control valves are essential in regulating fluid flow within various systems. Two-way motorized control valves feature two ports, allowing for on/off control of flow in a single direction, making them ideal for simple applications. Three-way motorized control valves have three ports, enabling mixing or diverting flows, thus offering more complex control suited for heating or cooling systems.
